About Us


DRY FRUITS PRODUCTS


Dry fruits have become an essential part of various occasions. From putting across a memorable wedding invitation to any puja ceremony, one can find their use in every such area. We, at Gouri Import and Export Company, are making an embellishing mark in the industry by providing a world-class array of Raisins, Cashew Nuts, and Pomegranates, since our inception in 2015. A Manufacturer, Supplier, and Exporter by nature, our goods are enriched with all the required nutrients, thus providing you with a soul-satisfying taste. Assuring you regarding our zero-preservative policy, our firm has left no stone unturned when it comes to delivering the best quality of seedless raisins, yellow raisins, golden raisins, black raisins and all types of cashew nuts to each of its customers. Using the most advanced tools for our production and processing operations, allows us to cater to bulk consignments in an efficient manner. Supported by a reliable packaging unit, our team uses a first-class range of materials to wrap these goods, thus protecting them from any kind of impurities


CHIA PRODUCTS


Today Chia is one of the most popular of superfoods and is the highest source of omega 3 fatty acids from a terrestrial source. The high nutrition profile has a composite presence of proteins, fatty acids, micronutrients, and dietary fibres. Chia seeds have been used for endurance running, fitness diets and part of general wellness food consumption by people across all ages. The CSIR-CFTRI agrotechnology based White Chia seeds has wonderful extensibility into food product designs with seamless integration into foods both fresh and processed


QUINOA PRODUCTS


Technically quinoa is a pseudo-cereal. It can seamlessly be substituted as a cereal in many dishes. It is comprehensive in nutrition, with a good profile of essential amino acids and micronutrients. The wonderful attribute of quinoa is its low glycaemic load, thus making it a good choice for those who need to control their blood sugar. From pediatrics and geriatrics, quinoa is a wonderful source of nutrition.


It is gluten-free and easy to digest, make our Quinoa part of your daily diet
